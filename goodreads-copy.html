Goodreads Copy

A copy of Goodreads, with which you can look up information about books, authors, as well as keep track of the books you're reading. You can also follow other users and add them as friends, which enables you to recommend books to them.

Your books are organized into shelves (either the default ones such as 'reading', which define the status of the book for you or a custom shelf you create). In addition, you can see book editions, sort by any of the attributes displayed on the shelves page and look for authors and books by their names.

Stack: HTML · CSS · JavaScript · ReactJS · Redux · Google Firebase Live | Repo
