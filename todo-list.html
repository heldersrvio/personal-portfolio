Todo List

A web application that lets you organize your todo items by priority, project, as well as customize them with checklists and notes. Items with different priorities are assigned different colors. You can also view all the items for the current date by clicking on the 'Today' option. The app also allows you to log in with Google and have your data saved.

Stack: HTML · CSS · JavaScript · Webpack · Google Firebase Live | Repo
