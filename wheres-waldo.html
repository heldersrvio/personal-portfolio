Weather App

Where's Waldo game for the web with 10 different scenarios and a leaderboard. First, you need to pick a scenario and then you'll have to locate Waldo, Wilma, Odlaw, the Wizard Whitebeard and Woof. If you manage to find all the characters in a short amount of time (which ranks you among the top 10 scorers), you'll be prompted to enter your name and join the leaderboard.

Stack: HTML · CSS · JavaScript · ReactJS · Google Firebase Live | Repo
